Sustainability

Minimizing Environmental Impact

We strive to minimize the environmental impact of our mining activities. We implement various environmental protection measures including the use of renewable energy, water resource management, and biodiversity conservation.

Community Development

Coexistence with local communities is our core value. We contribute to local community development through various social contribution activities such as educational support, medical service provision, and local infrastructure improvement.

Sustainability Goals

  • Achieve carbon neutrality by 2030
  • Expand renewable energy use to over 50%
  • Achieve 90% water reuse rate
  • Reach 100,000 beneficiaries through community education programs
