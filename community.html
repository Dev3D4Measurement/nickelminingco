Community & Commitment

Our Commitment to the Community

At PT.Sumber Bumi Putera, we believe that our success is intrinsically linked to the well-being of the communities where we operate. We are committed to fostering sustainable development and creating positive impacts that extend beyond our mining operations.

Community Development Programs

  • Education: Scholarships and school improvement projects
  • Healthcare: Mobile clinics and health education programs
  • Economic Empowerment: Local business development and skills training
  • Infrastructure: Road construction and maintenance
  • Environmental Conservation: Reforestation and biodiversity protection initiatives

Environmental Stewardship

We are dedicated to minimizing our environmental footprint through responsible mining practices, including:

  • Implementing advanced water management systems
  • Conducting regular environmental impact assessments
  • Investing in renewable energy sources for our operations
  • Comprehensive mine rehabilitation and closure planning
