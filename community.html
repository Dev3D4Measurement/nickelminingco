Community & Commitment

Our mission is to balance community demands

Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)

Knowing that for the mining industry CSR is about balancing the diverse demands of communities and the imperative to protect the environment with the ever present need to make a profit.

Also corporate social responsibility (CSR) is a key to communication with area community in an ethical way in the mining industry.

SBP continues to take responsibility for the communities surrounding our operations and the environment. We foster ethical communication with local communities and contribute to sustainable development.

Health Sector

  • Free health check-ups
  • Provision of medicines or medical devices to people in need
  • COVID-19 vaccination

We believe that health is a fundamental right, and our efforts aim to ensure that everyone in our vicinity has access to quality healthcare.

Education Sector

  • Scholarships for underprivileged or high-achieving students
  • Skills training
  • Seminars

We recognize the importance of education in empowering individuals and communities, and our programs are designed to support the educational aspirations of our stakeholders.

Environment Sector

Various activities aimed at environmental conservation, including promoting renewable energy use and enhancing waste management practices. We are committed to reducing our environmental footprint and promoting sustainable practices in all aspects of our operations.
