About SBP

Recently, the nickel mining industry has been gaining attention due to its connection with the production of batteries for electric vehicles, but most of Indonesian nickel is still mined and consumed as feed stock for nickel smelters for stainless steel production.

SBP mines nickel ore with a nickel content of 1.6-1.8% in a nickel mine located in Konawe Utara, Sulawesi Tenggara, together with a mining contractor, and supplies all of it to domestic nickel smelter plants.

SBP camp is located at Puusuli, KONAWE Utara, which houses the Site Office, staff and vendor staff accommodation, a restaurant, a work shop, and a fuel storage tank. A laboratory for ore analysis will also be established soon.
