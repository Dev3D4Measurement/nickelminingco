OPERATIONS

Production

  • In 2024, our target production is 800,000 tons.
  • The quality of nickel mined is 1.6-1.8%, with 1.4 as off-spec. Production is continuing in close cooperation with mining contractors and SBP's Technical Team.

Trucking and Stockpile Management

  • Nickel Ore mined from the Open Pit is transported to 8 stockpiles located around the PIT and Access road, where ETO and EFO are separated and stocked for shipment.
  • It is transported about 10 km on the access road and local road to the barge loading jetty.

Quality Control

  • We are preparing an on-site lab, which will be completed in November.
  • This will enable us to conduct our own analysis within our camp, getting results in just three or four hours, enabling efficient ore production and stock pile management.
